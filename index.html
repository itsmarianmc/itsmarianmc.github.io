About me
I am itsmarian, I'm a frontend website developer and active Minecraft player and I have my own Minecraft project in which I invest a lot of time. For this project I also run, manage and design a website as a hobby and for learning purposes. I create the websites to develop myself and to constantly expand my knowledge about websites, HTML, CSS, JavaScript in fields of frontend and backend. Among other things, I use artificial intelligence to test its limits and to help myself in tough situations in which I do not know further.
All the projects I make are open source and can be viewed on GitHub. You can use all of my games on this site to play by yourself or with your friends (for further information about Projekt City, please contact projektcityofficial@gmail.com or business.itsmarian@gmail.com).
Take a look around and maybe you'll be interested in one of my projects 😊

Projekt City [by SuffixHD & itsmarian]
Projekt City is a unique and expansive Minecraft project that operates without funding and is entirely free to play for everyone. Our mission is to develop an easily navigable Minecraft world for everyone, while paying a lot of attention to details and flawless work.

Timer, Stopwatch and World Clock Tools [by itsmarian]
Here you can experience various functions. There is a world clock that displays different times around the world live. A stopwatch with a start/stop, delete and lap system, all with an appealing and modern UI.
Open
Cookie Clicker [by itsmarian]
Cookie clicker is a very simple game where you get a cookie by pressing on a cookie! From this you can get upgrades, robots and other automations. If you have enough cookies, you can reset your progress to gain prestige and continue playing with a higher number of cookies per click.
Open
Block Blast [by itsmarian]
Block Blast is a relaxing puzzle game where you try to connect rows of blocks by placing them to fill a row. Try to make room for each piece so that you don't run out of space, otherwise the game is over.
Open
